About us

Streamlining Testing and Quality Assurance for High-Quality Electrical Products at SRS Electrical Appliances.

Welcome to the SRS Electrical Appliances Testing Management System. We manufacture high-quality electrical products like switch gears, fuses, capacitors, and resistors, ensuring their reliability through rigorous in-house testing before CPRI validation. Our new automated system streamlines this process, reducing errors and saving time by generating unique product and test IDs and organizing records modularly. It includes detailed remarks, advanced search functionality, and status tracking, enhancing efficiency and accuracy. This system helps us maintain our commitment to delivering top-notch electrical products.

  • Automated Record Generation
  • Advanced Search Options
  • Detailed Remarks
  • Status Tracking
